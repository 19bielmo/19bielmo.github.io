--- layout: post title: Choose Your Own Adventure: Find the Flies Caught in the World Wide Web! ---

The goal of this game is to choose your own adventure, and learn how to navigate the internet, also known as the Web!


  • Learning Goal: Learning how to navigate the web!
  • Setting: Both a web, and the internet. It's playing upon both meanings of web!
  • Things to Collect: Flies, before they are eaten by spiders
  • How to Win: Collect ALL of the flies
  • How to lose: Miss a fly and it gets eaten in the web
